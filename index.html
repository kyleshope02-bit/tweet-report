📞 Mag 7 Covered Calls — Income Strategy May 15 · -2d DTE · May 17, 2026

📋 How to read this tab
Deep ITM calls
Strike well below current price. Premium is mostly intrinsic value. High yield, maximum downside cushion, but you cap upside very close to today's price. Best if neutral-to-bearish short term. Rarely get called away at a loss if held to expiry.
Slight ITM / ATM calls
Strike near current price. Mix of intrinsic + time value. 1-4% yield for the month. Good balance: meaningful income, modest upside cap. Sweet spot for a monthly income strategy on quality stocks you want to hold.
OTM calls
Strike above current price. Pure time value — no intrinsic. Lower yield (1-3%) but stock keeps full upside up to the strike. Best if bullish but want some income. Stock must rally past strike to get called away.
Premiums (Black-Scholes)
Calculated using real IV data: MSFT 36% IV (96th pct — very elevated), META 38% IV (pre-earnings elevated). Mid = theoretical fair value. Bid is what you'll realistically fill at when selling. Always verify live chain in your broker before placing.
⚠ Earnings risk
Both earnings are before May 15 expiry. META: Apr 29 · MSFT: Apr 28. A strong beat can gap the stock above your strike → you miss upside. A miss can gap it below → losses mount. Premiums are fat partly because the market is pricing in this binary risk.
Key columns
Yield% = premium ÷ stock price
Breakeven = stock price − premium (your floor)
Eff. Sale = strike + premium (if called away)
Max P&L = (eff. sale − purchase price) × 100
Delta = probability call is ITM at expiry

📋 How alerts are generated
Synthetic Renko
ATR-based brick size (1.5× ATR-14). Each brick represents a fixed price move — filters out noise and shows pure trend direction. Green = uptrend, Red = downtrend.
EMA Compression (8/21/34)
Matt Bishop strategy: when the 8, 21, and 34 EMAs converge toward each other, energy is building for a directional move. Compression = spread narrowing >3% over 3 bars.
Squeeze Momentum
SQZMOM_LB: Bollinger Bands (2σ) inside Keltner Channel (1.5×ATR). When BB contracts inside KC, volatility is compressing. Breakout imminent.
🚨 ALERT Signal
Fires when ALL THREE align: (1) Renko flips red→green, (2) EMA 8/21/34 are converging, (3) Squeeze detected. Strongest setups have all three on the same bar.
