📋 Strategy — Renko-Driven Covered Calls
Why Renko first?
Only sell covered calls when the Renko is GREEN. Selling a call on a stock in a red Renko downtrend caps your upside and gives you no edge — you're fighting the trend. Wait for a green flip, then sell.
Which strike?
★★★ = +4% OTM sweet spot. Collects real premium (2-4%), gives the stock room to run with the Renko trend, and exits cleanly when Renko flips red. ATM if you want max income. Far OTM if very bullish.
When to exit the call?
When Renko flips RED on the screener tab — that's your signal to buy back the call and either close the stock or wait for the next green flip. Don't wait for expiry if Renko turns bearish first.
